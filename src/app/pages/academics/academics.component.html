Academics at Holy Redeemer

Our Curriculum & Programs

At Holy Redeemer High School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to foster intellectual growth and critical thinking. Explore our diverse programs tailored to meet the needs of every student.

Courses Offered

Part 1 - Tamil

Part 2 - English

Part 3 - A - General Education

  • Group 1: Maths, Physics, Chemistry, and Computer Science
  • Group 2: Maths, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Group 3: Physics, Chemistry, Botany, and Zoology
  • Group 4: Commerce, Accountancy, Economics, and History

Part 3 - B - Vocational Education

  • Group 1 - BEE: Maths
  • Group 2 - Accountancy and Auditing: Commerce

Note:

  • Part 1 and Part 2 are Compulsory
  • In Part 3-A, choose any one among the four groups
  • OR
  • In Part 3-B, choose any one between the two groups

Rules & Regulations

1. School Work Order

Students must adhere to the daily schedule and maintain discipline in all academic activities.

2. Leave

Leave will be granted only on prior application, except in emergencies, and must be approved by the class teacher.

3. Examination

Students must appear for all exams and follow the evaluation guidelines set by the school.

4. Uniform

Students must wear the prescribed uniform on all school days.

5. On Campus

Respect school property, follow the campus rules, and maintain cleanliness at all times.

6. General Conduct

Maintain discipline, respect teachers, and avoid any disruptive behavior.

7. Request to Parents

Parents are encouraged to support school policies and ensure students follow the guidelines set by the institution.
